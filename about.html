About Our Institution

Maharshi Arvind Vidya Mandir Varishth Madhyamik Vidyalaya is a premier educational institution located in the holy city of Kushinagar, Kasia. Established with the vision of providing value-based education, the school follows the philosophy of Sri Aurobindo and The Mother, focusing on the physical, vital, mental, and psychic development of every child.

Situated near the Mahaparinirvana Sthal of Lord Buddha, the school offers a serene environment conducive to learning. We are committed to academic excellence while ensuring that our students remain rooted in Indian culture and values.

Our Vision & Mission

Vision: To mold students into responsible citizens who are not only intellectually competent but also morally upright and socially responsive.

Mission: To provide a learning environment that encourages critical thinking, creativity, and discipline. We aim to bridge the gap between traditional wisdom and modern technology.
